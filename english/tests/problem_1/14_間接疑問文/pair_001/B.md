The field of psychology has evolved significantly since its origins in the late 19th century. Early researchers focused primarily on observable behavior, but modern psychologists are interested in the workings of the mind itself. **Understanding how emotions influence decision-making and why people sometimes act against their own interests has proven to be far more complicated than scientists initially expected.** These insights have applications in fields ranging from education to business management.
