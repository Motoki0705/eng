Advances in medical technology have raised difficult ethical questions that society is still struggling to answer. Gene editing techniques offer the promise of eliminating inherited diseases, but they also carry risks. **Doctors are often uncertain about what consequences new treatments might have in the long term, and deciding whether experimental procedures should be offered to patients remains a matter of intense debate.** These discussions will only become more complex as technology continues to advance.
