The global economy has undergone dramatic changes over the past few decades. Developing countries that once relied heavily on agriculture are now building manufacturing sectors. **The more rapidly a nation industrializes, the greater the environmental challenges it faces, and no country can develop as quickly as it might wish without paying some price in terms of pollution.** Finding a balance between economic growth and environmental protection remains one of the most pressing issues of our time.
