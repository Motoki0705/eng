## 問題

次の各英文の(   )に入れるのに最も適当な語(句)を、それぞれ①～④の中から1つ選びなさい。

**(a)** She is not (   ) much a teacher as a friend to her students.

① too
② very
③ so
④ more

**(b)** My English is not as (   ) as hers.

① good
② better
③ best
④ well

**(c)** This house is (   ) more expensive than the other one.

① very
② so
③ far
④ too

**(d)** The more you practice, (   ) you will play the piano.

① well
② better
③ the better
④ the best

**(e)** The news was none the (   ) surprising for being expected.

① more
② less
③ better
④ worse

**(f)** Not (   ) ten thousand people attended the festival.

① more than
② less than
③ fewer than
④ greater than

**(g)** I would stay home (   ) than go out in this weather.

① better
② more
③ rather
④ well

**(h)** He doesn't even know how to cook rice, (   ) prepare a full meal.

① much more
② much less
③ no more
④ no less
