## 問題

次の各英文の(   )に入れるのに最も適当な語(句)を、それぞれ①～④の中から1つ選びなさい。

**(a)** This movie is not (   ) as exciting as I expected.

① so
② very
③ much
④ more

**(b)** He is not so much a scholar (   ) a writer.

① than
② but
③ as
④ or

**(c)** Today's exam was (   ) easier than yesterday's.

① very
② so
③ much
④ more

**(d)** The harder you work, (   ) you will become.

① more successful
② the more successful
③ most successful
④ the most successful

**(e)** I like her none the (   ) for her faults.

① better
② less
③ more
④ worse

**(f)** There were (   ) five hundred people at the concert.

① no less than
② no more than
③ not less than
④ not more than

**(g)** She is a singer (   ) than an actress.

① better
② more
③ rather
④ well

**(h)** I can't speak French, (   ) German.

① still less
② still more
③ much more
④ no more
