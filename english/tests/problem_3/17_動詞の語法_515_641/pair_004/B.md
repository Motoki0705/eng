## 問題

**(a)** She gave up (   ) to lose weight after many failed attempts.

① try  ② trying  ③ to try  ④ tried

---

**(b)** Don't forget (   ) off the lights before you leave.

① turn  ② turning  ③ to turn  ④ turned

---

**(c)** The teacher let the students (   ) early today.

① leave  ② to leave  ③ leaving  ④ left

---

**(d)** I got my hair (   ) at the salon yesterday.

① cut  ② to cut  ③ cutting  ④ cutted

---

**(e)** He failed (   ) the deadline.

① meet  ② meeting  ③ to meet  ④ met

---

**(f)** The shirt you bought doesn't (   ) you very well.

① match  ② suit  ③ fit  ④ wear

---

**(g)** I can't (   ) that noise anymore. It's too loud.

① resist  ② stand  ③ hold  ④ keep

---

**(h)** The witness accused the man (   ) the crime.

① for  ② to  ③ of  ④ with
