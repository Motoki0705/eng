# 問題3 - 18_形容詞・副詞の語法 - pair_001 - A

**次の各文の空所に入れるのに最も適切なものを、①〜④の中から1つ選びなさい。**

---

**(a)** There is ( ) hope of his recovery from the illness.

① a few  
② a little  
③ few  
④ little  

---

**(b)** ( ) students attended the lecture because of the heavy rain.

① Few  
② A few  
③ Little  
④ A little  

---

**(c)** The movie was so ( ) that I watched it three times.

① interest  
② interested  
③ interesting  
④ interestingly  

---

**(d)** She was deeply ( ) at the news of his failure.

① disappoint  
② disappointing  
③ disappointed  
④ disappointment  

---

**(e)** These two problems are ( ) in nature.

① likely  
② alike  
③ like  
④ unlike  

---

**(f)** You should be more ( ) about other people's feelings.

① sensible  
② sense  
③ sensibly  
④ sensitive  

---

**(g)** Japan has become one of the most ( ) countries in the world.

① industrial  
② industrious  
③ industry  
④ industrially  

---

**(h)** I could ( ) believe what he said because it was so strange.

① hard  
② hardly  
③ harder  
④ hardest
