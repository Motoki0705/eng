# 問題3 - 18_形容詞・副詞の語法 - pair_001 - B

**次の各文の空所に入れるのに最も適切なものを、①〜④の中から1つ選びなさい。**

---

**(a)** ( ) people understand the importance of regular exercise.

① A little  
② Little  
③ A few  
④ Few  

---

**(b)** I have ( ) time to spare today, so I can help you.

① few  
② a few  
③ little  
④ a little  

---

**(c)** Are you ( ) in learning a foreign language?

① interest  
② interesting  
③ interested  
④ interestingly  

---

**(d)** It is ( ) that he will succeed in the examination.

① alike  
② like  
③ likely  
④ unlike  

---

**(e)** It was ( ) of you to bring an umbrella.

① sensitive  
② sensible  
③ sense  
④ sensibly  

---

**(f)** The ( ) workers finished the project ahead of schedule.

① industrial  
② industrious  
③ industry  
④ industrially  

---

**(g)** She studied so ( ) that she passed the exam with flying colors.

① hardly  
② hard  
③ harder  
④ hardness  

---

**(h)** The children were ( ) about the upcoming field trip.

① excite  
② exciting  
③ excited  
④ excitement
