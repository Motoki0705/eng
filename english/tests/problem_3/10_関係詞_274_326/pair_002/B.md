## 問題

次の各英文の(   )に入れるのに最も適当な語(句)を、それぞれ①～④の中から1つ選びなさい。

**(a)** The girl (   ) you met yesterday is my cousin.

① who
② whose
③ which
④ whom

**(b)** I don't understand the reason (   ) he was angry.

① which
② what
③ why
④ how

**(c)** There is no rule (   ) has no exception.

① which
② but
③ that
④ what

**(d)** I will give you (   ) information I have.

① what
② which
③ that
④ how

**(e)** He became a doctor, (   ) his father had been.

① that
② which
③ what
④ who

**(f)** This is the city (   ) I want to live.

① where
② which
③ that
④ in which

**(g)** The time will come (   ) you will regret this decision.

① where
② which
③ when
④ that

**(h)** She is what is (   ) a walking dictionary.

① called
② known
③ named
④ said
