## 問題

次の各英文の(   )に入れるのに最も適当な語(句)を、それぞれ①～④の中から1つ選びなさい。

**(a)** This is the house (   ) I was born.

① which
② where
③ that
④ what

**(b)** The man (   ) I thought was honest turned out to be a liar.

① which
② whom
③ who
④ whose

**(c)** He showed me the way (   ) he solved the problem.

① what
② where
③ which
④ how

**(d)** We got lost in the forest, (   ) made us very anxious.

① that
② what
③ which
④ it

**(e)** I gave him (   ) advice I could.

① what
② which
③ that
④ whose

**(f)** He is not (   ) he used to be.

① that
② which
③ what
④ who

**(g)** That is (   ) I came here.

① what
② why
③ which
④ how

**(h)** She has two daughters, both of (   ) are married.

① them
② who
③ whom
