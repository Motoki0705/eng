## 詳細な解説

### (a) I found it difficult (   ) solve the problem by myself.
**解答**: ③ to

**Next Stage参照**: No. 108 - S+V+it+C+to do

**解説**:
- S+V+it+C+to do の構文
- it は形式目的語

---

### (b) She lived (   ) be ninety years old.
**解答**: ① to

**Next Stage参照**: No. 112 - 副詞用法（結果）live to do

**解説**:
- live to do「...するまで生きる」
- 結果を表す副詞用法

---

### (c) It was kind (   ) you to help me with my homework.
**解答**: ② of

**Next Stage参照**: No. 117 - It is ... of A to do

**解説**:
- 人の性質を表す形容詞（kind）の場合は of を使う
- It is kind of you to do「...してくださって親切ですね」

---

### (d) The music was too loud for us (   ) concentrate.
**解答**: ③ to

**Next Stage参照**: No. 120 - too...to do

**解説**:
- too...for A to do「AがするにはあまりにA...すぎる」
- for us は不定詞の意味上の主語

---

### (e) He is rich (   ) to buy anything he wants.
**解答**: ④ enough

**Next Stage参照**: No. 121 - enough to do

**解説**:
- rich enough to do「...するのに十分金持ちだ」
- enough は形容詞の後に置く

---

### (f) I got up early (   ) order not to be late for school.
**解答**: ③ in

**Next Stage参照**: No. 125 - in order not to do

**解説**:
- in order not to do「...しないように」
- 目的を表す否定の不定詞

---

### (g) The meeting is (   ) be held next Monday.
**解答**: ③ to

**Next Stage参照**: No. 129 - be to 不定詞（予定）

**解説**:
- be to be held「開催される予定だ」
- be to 不定詞の予定用法

---

### (h) She can speak French, to say (   ) of English.
**解答**: ① nothing

**Next Stage参照**: No. 132 - to say nothing of A

**解説**:
- to say nothing of A「Aは言うまでもなく」
- = not to mention A
