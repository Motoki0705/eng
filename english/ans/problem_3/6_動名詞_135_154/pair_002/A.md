## 詳細な解説

### (a) My father objected to (   ) abroad alone.
**解答**: ② me going

**Next Stage参照**: No. 145 - object to doing

**解説**:
- object to doing「...することに反対する」
- me は動名詞の意味上の主語（my going も可）

---

### (b) She is accustomed to (   ) up early in the morning.
**解答**: ② getting

**Next Stage参照**: No. 144 - be accustomed to doing

**解説**:
- be accustomed to doing「...することに慣れている」
- to は前置詞なので後ろは動名詞

---

### (c) There seems to be no sense (   ) for such a long time.
**解答**: ③ in waiting

**Next Stage参照**: No. 141 - There is no sense (in) doing

**解説**:
- There is no sense (in) doing「...しても意味がない」
- in は省略可能だが、選択肢では in waiting が正解

---

### (d) He is proud of (   ) won the prize.
**解答**: ④ his having

**Next Stage参照**: No. 136, 137 - 動名詞の意味上の主語＋完了動名詞

**解説**:
- his は動名詞の意味上の主語
- having won は完了動名詞（過去の行為を表す）

---

### (e) It is no (   ) crying over spilt milk.
**解答**: ③ use

**Next Stage参照**: No. 139 - It is no use doing

**解説**:
- It is no use doing「...してもむだだ」
- ことわざ「覆水盆に返らず」

---

### (f) I'm looking forward to (   ) from you soon.
**解答**: ③ hearing

**Next Stage参照**: No. 142 - look forward to doing

**解説**:
- look forward to doing「...することを楽しみにする」
- to は前置詞なので後ろは動名詞

---

### (g) Have you ever had any difficulty (   ) a job?
**解答**: ② finding

**Next Stage参照**: No. 148 - have difficulty (in) doing

**解説**:
- have difficulty (in) doing「...するのが難しい」
- in が省略された形

---

### (h) (   ) arriving at the airport, she realized she had left her passport at home.
**解答**: ③ On

**Next Stage参照**: No. 154 - on doing

**解説**:
- on doing「...するとすぐに」
- = as soon as she arrived at the airport
