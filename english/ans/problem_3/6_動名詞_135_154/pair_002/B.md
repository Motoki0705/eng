## 詳細な解説

### (a) I don't feel like (   ) out tonight.
**解答**: ② going

**Next Stage参照**: No. 153 - feel like doing

**解説**:
- feel like doing「...したい気がする」
- like は前置詞なので後ろは動名詞

---

### (b) When it comes to (   ) a decision, he always hesitates.
**解答**: ② making

**Next Stage参照**: No. 146 - when it comes to doing

**解説**:
- when it comes to doing「話が...することになると」
- to は前置詞なので後ろは動名詞

---

### (c) She is busy (   ) ready for the party.
**解答**: ③ getting

**Next Stage参照**: No. 150 - be busy (in) doing

**解説**:
- be busy (in) doing「...するのに忙しい」
- in が省略された形

---

### (d) I'm not used to (   ) treated like this.
**解答**: ② being

**Next Stage参照**: No. 143 - be used to doing

**解説**:
- be used to doing「...することに慣れている」
- 受動態の動名詞 being treated

---

### (e) What do you say to (   ) a walk after dinner?
**解答**: ② taking

**Next Stage参照**: No. 147 - What do you say to doing?

**解説**:
- What do you say to doing?「...しませんか」
- to は前置詞なので後ろは動名詞

---

### (f) There is no (   ) what the weather will be like tomorrow.
**解答**: ② knowing

**Next Stage参照**: No. 151 - There is no doing

**解説**:
- There is no doing「...できない」
- = It is impossible to know / No one can know

---

### (g) She had trouble (   ) the right answer.
**解答**: ② finding

**Next Stage参照**: No. 149 - have trouble (in) doing

**解説**:
- have trouble (in) doing「...するのが難しい」
- in が省略された形

---

### (h) It goes without (   ) that practice makes perfect.
**解答**: ② saying

**Next Stage参照**: No. 152 - It goes without saying that...

**解説**:
- It goes without saying that...「...は言うまでもない」
- = Needless to say, ...
