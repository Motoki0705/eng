## 詳細な解説

### (a) I didn't believe the news (   ) he had passed the exam.
**解答**: ③ that

**Next Stage参照**: No. 338 - 同格の名詞節を導く that

**解説**:
- 同格の that 節「...という（ニュース）」
- the news の内容を説明する名詞節

---

### (b) He is not a teacher (   ) a scholar.
**解答**: ③ but

**Next Stage参照**: No. 333 - not A but B

**解説**:
- not A but B「AではなくB」
- 「教師ではなく学者だ」

---

### (c) (   ) A nor B is responsible for the accident.
**解答**: ③ Neither

**Next Stage参照**: No. 336 - neither A nor B

**解説**:
- neither A nor B「AもBも...ない」
- 動詞は B に一致

---

### (d) It was not long (   ) they found the lost child.
**解答**: ② before

**Next Stage参照**: No. 346 - It won't be long before...

**解説**:
- It was not long before...「まもなく...した」
- before 節は時の副詞節

---

### (e) I will go there (   ) it rains tomorrow.
**解答**: ② even if

**Next Stage参照**: No. 365 - even if...

**解説**:
- even if...「たとえ...でも」
- 譲歩を表す接続詞

---

### (f) (   ) had I left home when it began to rain.
**解答**: ① Hardly

**Next Stage参照**: No. 356 - Hardly had S done... when ~

**解説**:
- Hardly had S done... when ~「...するとすぐに～」
- 否定語が文頭で倒置

---

### (g) You cannot be too careful, (   ) you may lose your wallet.
**解答**: ① for

**Next Stage参照**: No. 357（類似）- 理由を表す接続詞

**解説**:
- for「というのは...だから」
- 理由を表す等位接続詞

---

### (h) He worked hard (   ) he might pass the examination.
**解答**: ② so that

**Next Stage参照**: No. 371 - so that S can...

**解説**:
- so that S may...「...するために」
- 目的を表す副詞節
