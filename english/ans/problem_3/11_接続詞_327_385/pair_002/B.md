## 詳細な解説

### (a) (   ) you should go or stay is up to you.
**解答**: ③ Whether

**Next Stage参照**: No. 340 - 文の主語となる whether 節

**解説**:
- Whether... or ~「...か～か」
- 主語となる名詞節を導く（if は不可）

---

### (b) I wonder (   ) he will come to the party.
**解答**: ② if

**Next Stage参照**: No. 339 - 動詞の目的語となる if 節

**解説**:
- if「...かどうか」
- wonder の目的語となる名詞節

---

### (c) (   ) the time you arrive, the meeting will have started.
**解答**: ③ By

**Next Stage参照**: No. 349 - by the time...

**解説**:
- by the time...「...するまでには」
- 主節は完了形（未来完了）

---

### (d) She walked slowly (   ) she should not slip on the ice.
**解答**: ① for fear that

**Next Stage参照**: No. 372以降 - for fear that...

**解説**:
- for fear that S should...「...しないように」
- 否定的な目的を表す

---

### (e) Child (   ) he is, he can speak three languages.
**解答**: ② as

**Next Stage参照**: No. 366 - 名詞 + as + S + V...（譲歩）

**解説**:
- 無冠詞名詞 + as + S + V...「...だけれども」
- 譲歩を表す構文

---

### (f) (   ) it may seem strange, it is true.
**解答**: ③ Although

**Next Stage参照**: No. 364 - although...

**解説**:
- although...「...だけれども」
- 譲歩を表す接続詞

---

### (g) You can take (   ) the bus or the subway to get there.
**解答**: ③ either

**Next Stage参照**: No. 335 - either A or B

**解説**:
- either A or B「AかBかどちらか」
- 選択を表す

---

### (h) It is ten years (   ) I last saw him.
**解答**: ④ since

**Next Stage参照**: No. 344 - It is... since ~

**解説**:
- It is... since ~「～してから...になる」
- 現在完了相当の意味
