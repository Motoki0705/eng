## 詳細な解説

### (a) The girl (   ) you met yesterday is my cousin.
**解答**: ④ whom

**Next Stage参照**: No. 277 - 目的格関係代名詞

**解説**:
- met の目的語となる目的格関係代名詞
- whom（口語では who も可、省略も可）

---

### (b) I don't understand the reason (   ) he was angry.
**解答**: ③ why

**Next Stage参照**: No. 287 - 関係副詞 why

**解説**:
- 理由を表す先行詞 the reason + 関係副詞 why
- = the reason for which he was angry

---

### (c) There is no rule (   ) has no exception.
**解答**: ② but

**Next Stage参照**: No. 313（または類似）- 擬似関係代名詞 but

**解説**:
- but = that...not（否定の関係代名詞）
- 「例外のない規則はない」

---

### (d) I will give you (   ) information I have.
**解答**: ① what

**Next Stage参照**: No. 303 - what A S+V...

**解説**:
- what information I have「私が持っているすべての情報」
- 関係形容詞 what

---

### (e) He became a doctor, (   ) his father had been.
**解答**: ② which

**Next Stage参照**: No. 292 - 非制限用法の which

**解説**:
- 補語（a doctor）を先行詞とする which
- 非制限用法

---

### (f) This is the city (   ) I want to live.
**解答**: ④ in which

**Next Stage参照**: No. 283, 284 - 前置詞 + which / 関係副詞 where

**解説**:
- live in the city → in which / where
- ① where も正解だが、選択肢として適切なのは ④

---

### (g) The time will come (   ) you will regret this decision.
**解答**: ③ when

**Next Stage参照**: No. 286 - 関係副詞 when

**解説**:
- 時を表す先行詞 the time + 関係副詞 when
- = the time at which you will regret

---

### (h) She is what is (   ) a walking dictionary.
**解答**: ① called

**Next Stage参照**: No. 307 - what is called A

**解説**:
- what is called A「いわゆるA」
- = what we call A / so-called A
