## 詳細な解説

### (a) She is not (   ) much a teacher as a friend to her students.
**解答**: ③ so

**Next Stage参照**: No. 185 - not so much A as B

**解説**:
- not so much A as B「AというよりはむしろB」
- 「教師というよりは友人だ」

---

### (b) My English is not as (   ) as hers.
**解答**: ① good

**Next Stage参照**: No. 179 - not as [so] + 原級 + as

**解説**:
- not as + 原級 + as「...ほど～ではない」
- as...as の間は原級

---

### (c) This house is (   ) more expensive than the other one.
**解答**: ③ far

**Next Stage参照**: No. 188 - much / far + 比較級

**解説**:
- far も比較級を強調「はるかに～」
- much, far, even, still, a lot など

---

### (d) The more you practice, (   ) you will play the piano.
**解答**: ③ the better

**Next Stage参照**: No. 192 - the 比較級..., the 比較級～

**解説**:
- the + 比較級..., the + 比較級～「...すればするほど、ますます～」
- 副詞の比較級 better

---

### (e) The news was none the (   ) surprising for being expected.
**解答**: ② less

**Next Stage参照**: No. 195 - none the 比較級 for

**解説**:
- none the less for A「Aだからといって少しも～ないわけではない」
- 「予期されていたからといって驚きは減らない」

---

### (f) Not (   ) ten thousand people attended the festival.
**解答**: ② less than

**Next Stage参照**: No. 200 - not less than A

**解説**:
- not less than A「少なくともA」
- 「少なくとも1万人が参加した」

---

### (g) I would stay home (   ) than go out in this weather.
**解答**: ③ rather

**Next Stage参照**: No. 203 - would rather A than B

**解説**:
- would rather A than B「BよりむしろAしたい」
- rather than の用法

---

### (h) He doesn't even know how to cook rice, (   ) prepare a full meal.
**解答**: ② much less

**Next Stage参照**: No. 205 - , much less...

**解説**:
- , much less...「ましてや...ない」
- 否定文の後に使用
