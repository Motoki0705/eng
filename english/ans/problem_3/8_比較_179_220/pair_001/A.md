## 詳細な解説

### (a) This movie is not (   ) as exciting as I expected.
**解答**: ① so

**Next Stage参照**: No. 179 - not as [so] + 原級 + as

**解説**:
- not so [as] + 原級 + as「...ほど～ではない」
- 否定文では so を使うことが多い

---

### (b) He is not so much a scholar (   ) a writer.
**解答**: ③ as

**Next Stage参照**: No. 185 - not so much A as B

**解説**:
- not so much A as B「AというよりはむしろB」
- = B rather than A

---

### (c) Today's exam was (   ) easier than yesterday's.
**解答**: ③ much

**Next Stage参照**: No. 188 - much + 比較級

**解説**:
- much は比較級を強調「はるかに～」
- very は比較級を修飾できない

---

### (d) The harder you work, (   ) you will become.
**解答**: ② the more successful

**Next Stage参照**: No. 192 - the 比較級..., the 比較級～

**解説**:
- the + 比較級..., the + 比較級～「...すればするほど、ますます～」
- 2つ目の節も the + 比較級

---

### (e) I like her none the (   ) for her faults.
**解答**: ② less

**Next Stage参照**: No. 195 - none the 比較級 for

**解説**:
- none the less for A「Aだからといって少しも～ないわけではない」
- 二重否定で「それでも好きだ」の意味

---

### (f) There were (   ) five hundred people at the concert.
**解答**: ③ not less than

**Next Stage参照**: No. 200 - not less than A

**解説**:
- not less than A「少なくともA」= at least A
- no less than は「Aも（たくさん）」

---

### (g) She is a singer (   ) than an actress.
**解答**: ③ rather

**Next Stage参照**: No. 203 - B rather than A

**解説**:
- B rather than A「AよりむしろB」
- = not so much A as B

---

### (h) I can't speak French, (   ) German.
**解答**: ① still less

**Next Stage参照**: No. 205 - , much less... / , still less...

**解説**:
- , still less...「ましてや...ない」
- 否定文の後に使用
