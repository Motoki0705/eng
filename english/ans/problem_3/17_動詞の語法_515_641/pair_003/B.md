## 詳細な解説

### (a) The company provides its employees (   ) free lunches.
**解答**: ③ with

**Next Stage参照**: No. 607 - provide A with B

**解説**:
- provide A with B「AにBを供給する」
- = provide B for A

---

### (b) The heavy rain stopped us (   ) going outside.
**解答**: ④ from

**Next Stage参照**: No. 605 - stop A from doing

**解説**:
- stop A from doing「Aが…するのを妨げる」
- = prevent A from doing

---

### (c) I can't thank you enough (   ) your kindness.
**解答**: ② for

**Next Stage参照**: No. 612 - thank A for B

**解説**:
- thank A for B「AにBのことで感謝する」

---

### (d) This train will take you (   ) the airport in 30 minutes.
**解答**: ③ to

**Next Stage参照**: No. 614 - take A to B

**解説**:
- take A to B「AをBに連れて行く」

---

### (e) Does this dress (   ) me? I'm not sure about the color.
**解答**: ③ suit

**Next Stage参照**: No. 619 - suit A

**解説**:
- suit A「（服装・色が）Aに似合う」（人に対して）
- fit A は「サイズが合う」

---

### (f) I'd like to (   ) these books from the library.
**解答**: ② borrow

**Next Stage参照**: No. 623 - borrow A

**解説**:
- borrow A「Aを（無料で）借りる」
- lend は「貸す」

---

### (g) The bad weather (   ) them from reaching the summit.
**解答**: ④ prevented

**Next Stage参照**: No. 604 - prevent A from doing

**解説**:
- prevent A from doing「Aが…するのを妨げる」

---

### (h) I (   ) solving that difficult problem is not easy.
**解答**: ③ doubt

**Next Stage参照**: No. 625 - doubt that 節

**解説**:
- doubt that 節「…であることを疑う」
- 文意：「その難しい問題を解くのは簡単ではないと思う」
