## 選択肢の正誤解説

| 番号 | 正誤 | 選択肢 | 解説 |
|:---:|:---:|--------|------|
| 11 | ✗ | Experts have reached a consensus that large language models possess genuine intelligence. | 本文では"experts continue to debate whether these impressive capabilities represent genuine intelligence or merely sophisticated pattern matching"とあり、議論が続いている。 |
| 12 | ○ | Training large AI models requires substantial amounts of energy. | 本文に"Training a state-of-the-art language model can consume as much electricity as several hundred homes use in a year"と明記。 |
| 13 | ✗ | Current AI systems can always distinguish between true and false information. | 本文では"they cannot distinguish between accurate information and convincing-sounding fiction"とあり、区別できない。 |
| 14 | ○ | Some medical institutions are using AI technology for diagnostic purposes. | 本文に"Some hospitals have begun using AI to help diagnose diseases by analyzing medical images"と明記。 |
| 15 | ✗ | AI is capable of handling both routine and creative tasks better than humans. | 本文では"AI can handle routine tasks more efficiently than humans, freeing people to focus on more creative and meaningful work"とあり、創造的な仕事は人間が行うとしている。 |
| 16 | ✗ | The automation of jobs will definitely create more employment opportunities than it eliminates. | 本文では"there is no guarantee that displaced workers will have the skills needed"とあり、保証はない。 |
| 17 | ✗ | All countries have agreed on a single approach to regulating AI technology. | 本文では"Different countries have adopted different approaches"とあり、アプローチは異なる。 |
| 18 | ○ | Finding a balance between innovation and safety is considered a major challenge. | 本文に"Finding the right balance between promoting technological progress and protecting society from potential harms remains one of the central challenges"と明記。 |
| 19 | ✗ | AI is only used in technology companies and has not spread to other industries. | 本文では病院、法律事務所など様々な分野での使用が言及されている。 |
| 20 | ○ | Environmental concerns about AI development have been raised by some researchers. | 本文に"their environmental impact has become a growing concern among researchers and environmentalists"と明記。 |

## 読解のポイント

### 1. 議論の両面を把握
- AI推進派：ルーチン作業の効率化
- 批判派：雇用喪失の懸念

### 2. 限定表現に注意
- "some" hospitals（一部の病院）
- "continue to debate"（議論が続いている）
